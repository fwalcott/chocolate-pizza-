For the fig swirl: Melt butter over medium heat with a saucepan. Add brown Sugar and stir to dissolve.Halve all the figs and toss into the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat and stir frequently, until yoy have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or too stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice Cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and grandulates sugar until sugar is completely disolved and the milk in barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture has cooled.

Using and ice cream machine, pour liquids into a frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding marcarpone, fig jam mixture, and then the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tuberware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
